Services & Portfolio

Specialty construction finishes.

From foundation to finish — here's what our skilled crews do best.

Stone

From natural fieldstone to cut limestone, we lay stone with precision and care — creating stunning, long-lasting exteriors that stand up to Canadian winters.

Brick

Traditional and modern brickwork executed with tight joints, clean lines, and the structural integrity your home or commercial property demands.

Block

Concrete block construction built to code — reliable, load-bearing walls for foundations, retaining structures, and commercial builds.

Stucco

Hand-applied and spray stucco systems that protect your exterior from moisture and the elements, while delivering a sharp, clean finish that lasts.

Wooden Wall

Custom wood wall installations — from shiplap and board-and-batten to full panelling systems, crafted and finished to complement any interior or exterior design.

Plaster

Interior and exterior plastering over lath or masonry — smooth, even coats applied with skill and patience for a refined, durable surface that holds its shape for decades.

Concrete

Residential and commercial concrete work including flatwork, footings, and structural pours — done right the first time, every time.

Interlocking Paver

Custom interlocking stone driveways, patios, and walkways that add curb appeal and hold up beautifully through freeze-thaw cycles year after year.

Outdoor Stairs

Safe, code-compliant outdoor stairways built from stone, brick, or concrete — with crisp edges, proper drainage, and solid footing you can count on.

Interior & Exterior Painting

Detailed surface preparation and professional-grade paint application inside and out — delivering a clean, lasting finish that protects your investment and impresses at first glance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers before we break ground.

What services do you specialize in?

We specialize in masonry, concrete, stone, brick, block, stucco, interlocking pavers, outdoor stairs, wooden wall plaster, and interior & exterior painting — serving residential and commercial clients across the Greater Toronto Area.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Sibel Construction is fully insured and licensed to operate in Ontario. We comply with all local building codes and safety regulations on every job site — giving you complete peace of mind.

Do you provide estimates?

Yes — we offer free, no-obligation on-site consultations and provide detailed written quotes so you know exactly what you're getting before any work begins. No surprises, no hidden costs.

How soon can you start?

Project start dates depend on scope and current scheduling. Contact us today and we'll work to accommodate your timeline — including fast-tracking urgent or time-sensitive projects where possible.
